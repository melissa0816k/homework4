Myths about organ donation

Myth: If I agree to donate my organs, the hospital staff won't work as hard to save my life.

When you go to the hospital for treatment, doctors focus on saving your life — not somebody else's. You'll be seen by a doctor whose specialty most closely matches your particular condition.
